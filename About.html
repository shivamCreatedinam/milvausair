ABOUT US

What is milvusair?

Milvus Air is an online platform where you can purchase flight tickets from various airlines. In addition to providing excellent service from booking to the final delivery of your tickets, Milvus Air also offers the option to pay with HRA Coin, giving you more flexibility in your payment options

We are delighted to present Milvus Air, a new company in the travel market that uses technological innovation as its strength to offer unique solutions.

Our offering is divided into three entities that form a complete ecosystem.

It starts with Milvus Air, our travel booking platform that offers rewards for every booking on our site.

And at the center is HRA Experience, our mobile app that enables travel to be booked using cryptocurrency and the possibility to pay through interest-free installments.

And last but not least is the HRA coin our cryptocurrency that supports our reward system and installment model.

OUR STORY

How it Started?

Out of passion for travel and the desire to meet pilgrims in particular for the very expensive pilgrimage, Milvus Air was born.

Milvus Air wants to distinguish itself by offering its passengers an interest-free installment model that is not only reserved for pilgrims but will also offer several financial benefits for the business traveller, big families and other adventurers.

"We go for limitless travel with financial peace of mind."
