HRA COIN

HRA coin is a cryptocurrency that supports our reward system.

Users earn HRA coin on every flight booked through Milvus Air, and can stake it for future bookings through our HRA Experience app.

HRA coin can be exchanged for euros or saved for later use on travel bookings. Our goal is to make HRA coin a tender for everyday and one-time purchases.
